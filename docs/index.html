How this works (in plain English)

This tool suggests whether to buy, sell, or hold palm oil futures over the next few days. It works like a weather forecaster for prices:
1It watches markets that move first
Palm oil rarely moves alone. Soybean oil (its main rival) and crude oil often move a day or two before it does. The tool watches those, plus palm oil's own recent moves.
2It learned from ~10 years of history
It studied thousands of past days and found patterns on its own — like “when these markets moved this way, palm oil usually went that way next.”
3It reads today's pattern
Each day it compares today's market moves to everything it learned, and estimates the chance the price goes up vs down over the next few days.
4It only acts when confident
A strong, clear pattern → Buy or Sell. A weak, unsure one → Hold and wait. It doesn't guess.
It is not a crystal ball. It is right a bit more often than a coin flip, and was tested only on data it had never seen. Its real strength is steering you away from big losses — not predicting every move.
This is an educational tool, not financial advice. It is still being tested and can be wrong. Please don't trade real money based on it without doing your own research. The numbers use data up to 2026-06-18. When the tool isn't confident, it suggests holding.
